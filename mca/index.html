  • The MCA course is approved by AICTE and affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya, Bhopal.
  • Since 2009, the department is getting good academic results. During the beginning of the sixth semester almost 75% of the MCA students got placed in Campus recruitments.
  • Some of the companies that offered placements to MCA students are Zensar Technologies, TCS, Infosys, Wipro Technologies, Global Logic, Hexaware Technologies, HCL, etc.
  • The department periodically organizes Guest lecturers/Workshops and other Faculty Development Programs for the benefit of students and faculties
  • The regular curriculum is supplemented by additional inputs to keep abreast of the changing scenario in the IT industry.
  • Further, project works and intradepartmental contests enable the students to sharpen their skills.
  • Students have also excelled in curricular and co-curricular activities and won laurels in competitions, intercollegiate fests
